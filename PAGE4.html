Evolution of E-commerce

  1. The notion of e-commerce first evolved in the 1970s with the introduction of electronic data interchange (EDI), which allowed firms to exchange documents electronically.
  2. The emergence of the World Wide Web in the 1990s cleared the door for the Internet's commercialization, resulting in the establishment of online retail behemoths such as Amazon and eBay.
  3. Mobile commerce (m-commerce), which allows customers to shop using their smartphones and tablets, first appeared in the early 2000s.
  4. Today, e-commerce is evolving through innovations such as social commerce, subscription-based services, and the incorporation of artificial intelligence and machine learning technology.

E-commerce has revolutionized the way people purchase, providing ease, accessibility, and a diverse choice of items and services at their fingertips.
